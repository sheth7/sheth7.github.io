Ananya Sheth

PhD Candidate

Purdue University

I am a PhD candidate at Purdue University’s Innovation Science Laboratory. My work focuses on better understanding the tension between risk management and growth in enterprises with the objective of building resilient organizations. Specific research interests include understanding enterprise risks as complex networks and pursuing systematic, repeatable innovation using our lab’s innovation toolkit.

A proposal to study enterprise risk management using complexity theory is currently in review at the National Science Foundation.
